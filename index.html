What is ICT?

Information and Communication Technology (ICT) is a broad and encompassing field that plays a pivotal role in our modern world. It encompasses various technologies, including computers, the internet, telecommunications, and digital devices, to facilitate the storage, retrieval, transmission, and exchange of information. ICT is fundamental in our daily lives, impacting education, business, healthcare, entertainment, and more. It empowers global connectivity, enabling instant communication and collaboration across borders. With ongoing advancements, ICT continues to shape the way we live, work, and interact, driving innovation and reshaping industries while bridging geographical and cultural gaps.

Current industry on IT

The current IT industry is significantly different from the past. As IT evolves, the industry adapts to technological advancements. With the increasing popularity of AI, IT is no longer solely about programming; it values a broader skill set. In earlier times, the industry didn't emphasize skills like presentation, leadership, and teamwork. Today, these skills are highly valued. Programming tasks have become more accessible, but skills like those mentioned cannot be replicated by AI, making them uniquely human. As a result, the industry now seeks individuals with a combination of skills and knowledge, recognizing the importance of both in the rapidly changing technology landscape.
